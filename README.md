# 💫 About Me:
👋 Hello, my name is Lassad KEFI and I'm a full-stack developer based in Paris 🇫🇷. My passion lies in creating innovative solutions to complex problems 💡, and I enjoy exploring new technologies to help me achieve this goal 🚀. <br>

Most of my development work revolves around Laravel 🔨 and Vue.js or Nuxt.js 🌐, but I'm also interested in working with Node.js 🟢, Nest.js 🐦, and Python 🐍. I believe that being well-versed in multiple programming languages and frameworks allows me to approach problem-solving from different angles, which ultimately leads to better results 👨‍💻.<br>

As a freelancer, I've had the opportunity to work with many different companies and clients, which has allowed me to develop a diverse skill set and work on a variety of projects 🤝. Some of my most notable accomplishments include API development 🌐, e-learning system 🎓, automated invoice generation 💰 and banking system 💳.<br>

When I'm not coding, you can usually find me working out 💪 or playing video games 🎮 (Let me know if you play PUBG 😉). I'm always looking for new challenges and opportunities to grow as a developer, and I believe that learning never stops 🧠.<br>

If you're interested in working with me or just want to say hi, feel free to reach out 📩. I'm always excited to collaborate on new projects and connect with fellow developers 🤝. Thank you for taking the time to read my about me! 😊<br>
